Marcus Voice Assistant

Marcus is a voice activated AI assistant, using tools from OpenAI and Microsoft Azure.

What sets it apart from other voice assistants is, that it implements ChatGPT for behavior that isn't pre-defined (i.e. asking for the weather or playing music). It uses requests to ChatGPT instead of making requests to any search engine or other website. That way results can vary in wording and even explained afterwards, simply by asking Marcus to explain further.

Marcus will be able to do everything an assistant should be able to do: he will play music, tell you the weather, set alarms for you and more!

He features a realistic voice using OpenAI's Text-To-Speech model.

Conversations are saved for up to an hour to really get a feeling of having a conversation.

Simply say "Marcus" and start talking!

ChatGPT

ChatGPT is an AI-text-model developed by OpenAI. The latest version is ChatGPT 4o.

  • Understanding human speech
    • ChatGPT is capable of understanding and interpreting human speech in multiple different languages and dialects.
  • Replicating human speech
    • ChatGPT is able to replicate human speech to the degree that it can use dialects and informal language as well as talk in other languages.
  • Context
    • ChatGPT takes the context from your conversation to be able to give you the best possible answer.
  • Knowledge
    • ChatGPT's knowledge base goes up to October 2023 in its latest version from May 2024
