Legends of Aitera

Legends of Aitera is a new type of MMORPG designed to address a plentitude of issues with the overall genre. Our design philosophy is RPG first, MMO second. In other words we want to make a game that prioritizes world building, story and role playing with multiplayer as a bonus.

The game itself is still in early stages of development. The website is also in development, but basic parts are already accessible.

Currently the website features a main page with an overview of the game as well as a basic about page and a development blog. Future features will include a forum, community polls as well as an on-site wiki page!
