LX-IO

Welcome to the official website of LX-IO. LX-IO is a project by lythox, who came up with the idea, of publishing software under this name.

The name consists of two parts, LX and IO. LX are the first and last letter of his pseudonym. IO is a common computer science acronym which means Input - Output and is being used as a generic term for all software.
